Mapo Tofu

Description

Mapo Tofu is a popular Chinese dish from Sichuan province known for its spicy and flavorful sauce. It features soft tofu cubes cooked in a savory and spicy sauce made with fermented black beans, chili paste, and Sichuan peppercorns. Mapo Tofu is typically served with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • Firm tofu
  • Ground pork (optional)
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ginger, minced
  • Fermented black beans
  • Chili paste or doubanjiang
  • Soy sauce
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Cornstarch
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Drain and cut the tofu into cubes.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or skillet and saut√© minced garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add ground pork (if using) and cook until browned.
  4. Add fermented black beans, chili paste, and soy sauce to the pan and stir well.
  5. Add chicken or vegetable broth and bring to a simmer.
  6. Gently add the tofu cubes to the sauce and simmer until heated through.
  7. Thicken the sauce with a cornstarch slurry if desired.
  8. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot with steamed rice.
