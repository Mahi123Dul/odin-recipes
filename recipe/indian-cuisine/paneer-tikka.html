Paneer Tikka

Description

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made with marinated paneer cubes, bell peppers, onions, and spices. The marinated paneer and vegetables are skewered and grilled or baked until golden brown and flavorful. Paneer Tikka is often served with mint chutney and makes for a delicious starter or snack.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Bell peppers (capsicum)
  • Onions
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (e.g., cumin, coriander, garam masala)
  • Lemon juice
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cut paneer, bell peppers, and onions into cubes.
  2. Prepare the marinade by mixing yogurt, spices, and lemon juice.
  3. Marinate paneer and vegetables in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Skewer the marinated paneer and vegetables onto skewers.
  5. Grill or bake the skewers until paneer is golden brown and vegetables are tender.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.
