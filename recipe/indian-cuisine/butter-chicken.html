Butter Chicken

Description

Butter Chicken is a classic Indian dish known for its creamy and flavorful sauce. Tender chicken pieces are marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices, then cooked in a rich tomato-based sauce with butter and cream. This dish is best served with naan bread or steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (e.g., cumin, coriander, garam masala)
  • Tomato paste
  • Butter
  • Cream

Step

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt and spices for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook the marinated chicken in a pan until lightly browned.
  3. In a separate pan, saut√© tomato paste and butter.
  4. Add the cooked chicken to the tomato sauce and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Stir in cream and simmer for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with naan bread or steamed rice.
