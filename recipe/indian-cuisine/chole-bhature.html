Chole Bhature

Description

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with deep-fried bread (bhature). The chickpea curry is made with a blend of aromatic spices, onions, tomatoes, and cooked until thick and flavorful. Bhature, a soft and fluffy fried bread, pairs perfectly with the spicy chole and is often enjoyed as a hearty breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (garbanzo beans)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Spices (e.g., cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala)
  • Yogurt
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight and pressure cook until soft.
  2. Saut√© onions, ginger, and garlic in oil until golden brown.
  3. Add spices, tomatoes, and cooked chickpeas to the pan and simmer until thickened.
  4. Prepare dough for bhature and let it rest for 1 hour.
  5. Divide the dough into small balls and roll out into circles.
  6. Deep fry the bhature in hot oil until golden brown and puffed up.
  7. Serve hot with chole, sliced onions, and lemon wedges.
