Masala Dosa

Description

Masala Dosa is a popular South Indian dish consisting of a thin, crispy rice crepe filled with a spicy potato filling. The dosa batter is made from fermented rice and lentils, giving it a tangy flavor and light texture. Masala Dosa is typically served with coconut chutney and sambar, a flavorful lentil stew.

Ingredients

  • Dosa batter (fermented rice and lentil batter)
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Green chilies
  • Curry leaves
  • Mustard seeds
  • Turmeric powder
  • Ghee or oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the potato filling by saut√©ing onions, green chilies, and mustard seeds.
  2. Add boiled and mashed potatoes to the mixture, along with turmeric powder and salt.
  3. Heat a dosa pan and spread a ladleful of dosa batter thinly on the pan.
  4. Add a spoonful of the potato filling to the center of the dosa.
  5. Drizzle ghee or oil around the edges of the dosa and cook until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Fold the dosa in half or roll it up and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
